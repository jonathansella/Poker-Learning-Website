Poker Legends

Phil Ivey

"The Tiger Woods of Poker"

Known for his incredible reads and fearless play style, Phil Ivey is widely considered the best all-around poker player in history.

10 WSOP bracelets • Over $32M in tournament earnings • Member of the Poker Hall of Fame

Doyle Brunson

"Texas Dolly"

A true pioneer of poker, Doyle helped transform the game from a backroom activity to a mainstream phenomenon.

10 WSOP bracelets • Author of "Super System" • 50+ year career • Poker Hall of Fame inductee

Phil Hellmuth

"The Poker Brat"

Famous for his larger-than-life personality and incredible tournament success, particularly in WSOP events.

16 WSOP bracelets (World Record) • 1989 Main Event Champion • Known for epic "Hellmuth rants"

Daniel Negreanu

"Kid Poker"

One of poker's most popular players, known for his ability to read opponents and verbalize their exact holdings.

6 WSOP bracelets • 2 WPT titles • Over $42M in tournament earnings

Antanas Guoga

"Tony G"

Famous for his table talk and aggressive playing style, Tony G is one of poker's most entertaining characters.

Over $6M in tournament earnings • High Stakes cash game regular • Known for legendary table banter

Tom Dwan

"durrrr"

Online poker phenomenon turned high-stakes legend, known for his fearless bluffs and unconventional playing style.

Online poker pioneer • High Stakes cash game specialist • Famous for the "durrrr Challenge"

Patrik Antonius

"The Finn"

Former tennis player and model turned poker pro, known for his stoic demeanor and incredible success in the highest stakes games.

Over $12M in tournament earnings • High Stakes cash game legend • Online poker record holder

Jennifer Harman

"The First Lady of Poker"

Pioneer for women in poker, known for her success in the highest stakes cash games and tournaments.

2 WSOP bracelets • Poker Hall of Fame inductee • Regular in "The Big Game"

Jonathan Sella

"King John"

A true poker prodigy, known for his incredible hero calls and uncanny ability to read opponents. His meteoric rise in the poker world has established him as one of the most promising talents in the game.

Rising star in high-stakes games • Famous for hero calls • Known for exceptional hand reading abilities

Gus Hansen

"The Great Dane"

Known for his ultra-aggressive style and unconventional play, Hansen revolutionized tournament poker in the early 2000s.

3 WPT titles • 1 WSOP bracelet • Author of "Every Hand Revealed"

Phil Laak

"The Unabomber"

Known for his eccentric personality and trademark hoodie, Laak brings mathematical precision and endless energy to the poker table.

1 WSOP bracelet • WPT title holder • World Record for longest poker session (115 hours)
